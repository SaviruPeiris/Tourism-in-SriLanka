Maldives

Republic of the Maldives is an island nation in the Indian Ocean.

It is composed of multiple atolls which are made up of a total of 1196 islands,

and is located in the continent of Asia.

The chain of islands is to the south-west of India and Sri Lanka and stretches over 871 km.
